Our Heritage

Founded and led by Narayan Singh, BDC Industries (Balaji Die Casting Industries) has established itself as a premier manufacturer and supplier in the gas stove and die-casting sector.

Located in the industrial hub of Samaypur Badli, New Delhi, our state-of-the-art facility specializes in producing all types of mixing tubes, high-grade LPG gas stoves, and durable burner components.
